Create. Collaborate. Empower.

GOAT was created as an open source collaboration website for educators to share notes, lessons, and collaborate. In this community you can create content and use content to help enrich your lessons and empower students.

Content is availible with and without an account but, by linking GOAT4Empowerment with your email account, you can comment, like, and collaborate with other instructors.
